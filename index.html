Our Past

The History of Happy Burger

From Chihuahua

Originally hailing from Chihuahua, Mexico, our dedicated chefs meticulously recreate traditional dishes that celebrate the rich culinary traditions of our homeland. Every ingredient is carefully selected to ensure the highest quality and authenticity, capturing the essence of Mexican cuisine in each and every bite. From sizzling burgers to flavorful enchiladas, our menu is a testament to the time-honored recipes that have been cherished by our family for generations. Each dish is prepared with passion and attention to detail, showcasing the vibrant flavors and spices that make Mexican cuisine so beloved worldwide.

To El Paso

At Happy Burger, we take immense pride in sharing our family's heritage with the El Paso community for over 25 years. We are honored to bring the true flavors of Chihuahua to your table and invite you to join us in celebrating the rich tapestry of Mexican cuisine. Whether it's a casual lunch with loved ones or a special occasion, we are excited to welcome you into our family and serve you an authentic taste of Mexico.

Quality

Our talented team of skilled chefs ensures that every bite is a culinary delight, leaving you craving for more. At Happy Burger, we believe that food brings people together, and we strive to create a warm and welcoming environment. Come and experience the warmth, flavors, and traditions of Happy Burger. We can't wait to serve you!
